Tap Duel

Tap Duel is the ultimate test of reflexes. Play face-to-face on one device, online via Game Center, or solo to sharpen your reaction time. Perfect for quick bursts of competitive fun!

Role: Developer & Designer Tech: Swift, Xcode, SwiftUI, RevenueCat, GameKit Year: 2025
  • Real-Time Online Multiplayer – Face off against friends or global opponents using Game Center.
  • Local Two-Player Mode – Play head-to-head on the same device—perfect for quick showdowns.
  • Solo Reflex Trainer – Sharpen your reaction time with a solo challenge mode.
  • Quickfire Rounds – “First to 5” wins! Every tap counts in these short, high-stakes battles.
  • Timing is Everything – Tap the icon the moment it appears… but tap too early and you lose the point!
  • Customizable Visuals – Unlock and equip icons, and premium packs (cats, memes, holidays & more).
  • Track Your Stats – Dive into detailed performance analytics including fastest taps, win streaks, and more.
  • Fun for Everyone – Simple to learn, addictive to master. Great for all ages and skill levels.

Ready to prove who’s the fastest tapper? Download Tap Duel and claim your bragging rights.
